Technology

Modern life is completely dependent upon technology. People are totally reliant on technology and is incomplete without it. Technology plays a significant role in every part of human life. Technology can be defined as the application of scientific knowledge to the practical aims of human life. By making the means of communication and transportation faster it has made the lives of people simple and comfortable. Without technology, existence of life would be almost impossible. Transportation, communication, education, health and business are some areas where technology is helpful. It also has significant negative effects. Over use of screens and the sedentary behavior can lead to physical health issues such as eye strains. Automation and artificial intelligence (AI) can lead to the job displacement of many people as certain tasks and roles has become automated.

- Positive Aspects of Technology -

Communication

Technology has contributed the most in the field of communication. Through the platforms like social media, messaging apps and emails, technology enables quick and continent communication between people all over the world. It has improved communication through the internet and mobile devices. In 1875, Graham Bell made the first technological advancement that had an impact on communication: the telephone. One technological invention that changed communication was mobile phone.

Health

The medical industry has benefited a lot from technology. Because of technology the understanding of the diseased has been improved significantly. Furthermore, the disease prevention and health promotion also has been significantly increased. The development of vaccines against several diseases is also possible due to the advancement of technology. Technology helped the development of several vaccines because without it, the certain diseases would still be killing large numbers of people.

Education

Technology also has a big impact on educational field. By the use of various techniques, the teaching and learning process has been significantly improved. The students can learn through online institution and classroom with the help of digital books. It has improved the process of teaching by introducing new methods that facilitate connected teaching. Through this technique the teachers can deliver the course material and learning resources effectively. Students and teachers can get connected not only physically but digitally through this platform

Business

Technology is being used in the business industry to increase productivity and improve efficiency. Technology facilitates seamless communication and collaboration within the team, regardless of geographical boundaries. The business are reached worldwide through internet and e-commerce platform. It also help business reduce costs through automation. By offering scalable infrastructure, adaptable software solutions, and digital marketing platforms to reach a wider audience, technology helps firms develop and grow more quickly.

- Negative Aspects of Technology -

Even though technology has many positive aspects, it also have a negative effect on the society because it affects the communication and alters how people perceive social interaction. Nowdays, there are a ton of dating websites on the internet that are populated by individuals looking for friends and lover. It has also affected the economic status of the families. Nowdays, analog cameras has been replaced with digital camera due to their simplicity and effectiveness. There are several medical technologies such as stem cell research and gene therapy which are against the moral and ethics of some religious people. Automation and artificial intelligence(AI) can lead to the job displacement of many people as certain tasks and roles has become automated. Over use of screens and the sedentary behavior can lead to physical health issues such as eye strains and sleep disruptions. The disposal of technology devices can contribute to electronic waste generation which can harm the environment
