About Us

Clicks is an e-commerce website based in Kathmandu, Nepal, specializing in the sale of cameras from renowned brands such as Canon, Nikon, and Sony. As brand partners of these companies, we have been operating since 2022 and currently consist of a team of four members. Our primary objective is to provide a comprehensive range of camera-related products, ensuring that our customers can benefit from the convenience and variety available on our website. By offering products from different brands, we aim to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of our customers. Whether they are professional photographers, hobbyists, or individuals looking to capture special moments, our goal is to provide them with a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. With a commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, Clicks strives to become a trusted destination for camera enthusiasts in Nepal and beyond.
